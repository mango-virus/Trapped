Labyrinth of Horrors

How to Play

You are trapped inside a shifting labyrinth. Find the key and escape through the exit door on every level. Monsters hunt you. Traps kill you. You only get one life per run.

Rules

  • You start with 100 HP, a flashlight (slot 1) with limited battery, and max 5 items.
  • Pick up batteries to recharge your flashlight. A reserve battery is kept if yours is already full.
  • Med packs heal. Use them by left-clicking while selected.
  • First player to escape a level gets the best pick of 3 buffs / items. Later escapees get lesser options — sometimes with a debuff attached.
  • Dead players become monsters on the next level — hunt the living.
  • Deeper levels spawn more monsters, more traps, and new themes.
